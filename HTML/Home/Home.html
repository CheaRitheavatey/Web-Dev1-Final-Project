Fourth Wing

by Rebecca Yarros | Fantasy


In the soaring world of Rebecca Yarros' Fourth Wing, Violet Sorrengail, a young scribe with a defiant spirit, must confront the ruthless world of dragon riders, where only the strongest survive. Amidst brutal training and treacherous politics, Violet must forge an unbreakable bond with a powerful dragon to fulfill her destiny and save her kingdom.
